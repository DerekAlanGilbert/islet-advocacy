Build a single-page advocacy website called "Diabetic Collective" -- a diabetes advocacy platform currently focused on the ISLET Act and islet transplant research awareness.

Tech: Plain HTML + CSS + vanilla JS. Single index.html file. Clean, modern design. No frameworks.

## Brand
- Name: Diabetic Collective
- Tagline: "Advocating for a cure. Together."
- Color scheme: Professional, warm but serious. Teal/blue-green primary with warm accents.

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero / About Section
- Diabetic Collective branding
- Brief mission statement about diabetes advocacy
- Currently focused on the ISLET Act

### 2. The Science Section
- Explain tegoprubart (Eledon Pharmaceuticals) trial at University of Chicago
- Anti-CD40L antibody for islet transplant immunosuppression (replacing tacrolimus)
- Results: 6/6 patients achieved insulin independence, HbA1c under 6%, no severe hypos, 15+ months stable
- Trial expanding to 12 subjects, funded by Breakthrough T1D (formerly JDRF)
- Second study funded for T1D + chronic kidney disease patients
- Why this matters: traditional immunosuppression has harsh side effects

### 3. The ISLET Act Section
- What: Reclassify cadaveric islets from "biologic drugs" to "organs" under Public Health Service Act
- Why: Simplifies regulation, improves insurance coverage, aligns US with Canada/EU/Australia/Japan
- Sponsors: Sen. Mike Lee (R-UT) and Sen. Ted Budd (R-NC)
- Status: Referred to Senate HELP Committee (Nov 2025)
- Mention Breakthrough T1D perspective that HHS could also do this administratively

### 4. Take Action Section
- Pre-written message template (copyable) for contacting senators about the ISLET Act
- Template should be professional, mention the bill by name, include talking points, have placeholder for personal T1D connection
- "Copy Message" button that copies to clipboard
- Instructions: "Find your senator below, click Contact, and paste this message"

### 5. Senator Table
- ALL 100 US Senators
- Columns: Name, Party (R/D/I), State, Phone, Committee, Actions
- Phone numbers clickable (tel: links)
- "Contact" button linking to their web contact form (opens new tab)
- HELP Committee members sorted to TOP and highlighted with a distinct background
- Bill sponsors (Lee, Budd) get a special "Sponsor" badge
- HELP Committee Chair (Cassidy) and Ranking Member (Sanders) noted
- Search/filter box (by name, state, party)
- Mobile responsive (cards on mobile, table on desktop)

### 6. Footer
- Diabetic Collective name
- Disclaimer that this is a grassroots advocacy effort
- Links to key resources (Eledon Pharmaceuticals, Breakthrough T1D, ISLET Act text)
